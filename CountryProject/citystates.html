Cities and States

The United States includes 50 states and several territories. The 5 largest cities are:

  1. New York, New York
  2. Los Angeles, California
  3. Chicago, Illinois
  4. Houston, Texas
  5. Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
The 5 largest states are:
  1. California
  2. Texas
  3. Florida
  4. New York
  5. Pennsylvania

California is the most populated state, with about 40 million residents. Located on the west coast of the United States, the state of California joined the USA in 1850.
Texas is the second most populated state, with about 29 million residents! It is on the southern side of the USA, and joined in 1845.
Florida is the third most populated state, with about 22 million residents! It is on the south-east side of the USA, and joined in 1845.
New York is the foruth most populated state, with about 20 million residents! It is on the east side of the USA, and joined in 1788. It was one of the original 13 colonies of the USA. It also is one of the buisiest states, and holds many attractions such as the Statue of Liberty.
Pennsylvania is the fifth most populated state. The state population is around 18 million. It is also on the east side, and it joined in 1787. It was also a colony, and used to be the capital of the USA. It includes relics such as the Liberty Bell.
